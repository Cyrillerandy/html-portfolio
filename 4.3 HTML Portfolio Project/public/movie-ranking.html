Must watch movies: A review by Cyrille Omondi.

My top 3 movies of all time.

Dune

Cinematography masterpiece with a beautifully crafted storyline.

Manchester by the sea.

A story of pain, loss and healing. A must watch for all.

The Infinity saga.

A series of movies to be precise, but the build up from the first installment of the franchise in 2008 to the epic conclusion in 2021 makes this one of the greatest sci-fi stories ever told.
